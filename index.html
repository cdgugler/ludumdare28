You are Orange bear. You like bananas, honey, and purple bear. You need to get to the exit, but you can't jump! Luckily, your friend Sharpy can help pull you to the top, just be careful about getting too close. Grab your favorite item (you only get one), and try to make it to the exit. You are impulsive, so if you walk by another item you will toss your old item away.

TLDR; Click on the screen and the Orange Bear will move towards the sharp spiked sprite until the energy bar in the top left is gone. Get the bear to the end as fast as possible for the highest score. Speed + least mount of destroyed items will award the highest score. Exit while holding the purple bear for an even higher score.
